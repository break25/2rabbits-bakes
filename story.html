Story

Est. Koramangala, Bangalore

We didn't open a bakery. We opened a feeling.

Scroll to find out how it started ↓
01

The Beginning

It started with too much butter and not enough space.

Two people. One tiny kitchen in Koramangala. The idea was simple — make things so good that people wouldn't need a reason to visit. Just hunger, and a little curiosity about the pink door.

02

The Name

Why two rabbits? Because one was never going to be enough.

The name came from a late night, a bad pun, and the realization that everything worth doing is better with someone else. Two rabbits. Two people. One shared obsession with cheesecake.

03

The Pink

The walls were an accident. Then they became everything.

We painted them on a Sunday. We weren't sure about the colour. Then the first customer walked in, stopped at the door, and said — 'oh this is exactly what I needed today.' The pink stayed.

04

Right Now

Still here. Still baking. Still a little obsessed.

Every morning the kitchen starts before the city wakes up. Nothing is frozen. Nothing is made yesterday. We make it because we love it, and because Koramangala deserves something that tastes like someone cared.
