Our Story

Two sisters. One pink dream kitchen.

What started as weekend baking sessions between two sisters has blossomed into a haven of handmade sweetness. Every cake, tart, and frosted cookie is crafted with care, bringing our recipes from our home to yours.

We believe in the magic of small-batch baking. Everything is made fresh to order, ensuring that you receive a dessert that is not only beautiful but truly made just for you.
